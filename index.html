about us

Together we can save the planet

At Green, we envision a world where nature thrives and communities live in harmony with the environment. We believe that a healthy planet is essential for a prosperous future, and we are dedicated to making that vision a reality. Founded in 2006, we are a passionate team of environmentalists, educators, and community advocate. Our diverse backgrounds and expertise drive innovative solutions to the pressing environmental challenges we face today. Subscribe to our newsletter for more..

At Green, we envision a world where the beauty and diversity of our natural environment thrive harmoniously alongside human progress. Our mission is to inspire and empower individuals, communities, and organizations.Together, we aim to create a global movement that prioritizes environmental stewardship, promotes biodiversity, and champions sustainable living for all.

Our Mission

  • We believe in the power of collective action. Our mission is to:
  • Educate: Raise awareness about environmental issues and inspire action.
  • Empower: Provide resources and tools for individuals and communities to make a positive impact.
  • Engage: Mobilize people across the globe to advocate for policies that protect our environment.
